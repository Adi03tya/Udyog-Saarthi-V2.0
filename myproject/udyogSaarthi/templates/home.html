Our Services

Job Mapping

To explore suitable job roles for persons with disability across sectors.

Accessibility Audit

To assess Physical accessibility of a worksite and suggest alterations to achieve the same.

Sensitization Workshop

To create awareness and attitudinal accessibility at different levels of the workforce in an organisation.

Sign Language Interpreter

It involves supporting hiring organizations with Sign Language Interpreter for Induction training of PwD workforce (Hearing Impaired or Hearing Hard).

Secondary Training

It involves anticipating and building competencies for future needs to equip the PwD workforce with the required skill set for career development and growth.

Return to Work

Every worker has a probability to acquire a disability or illness that hinders their ability to work. Thus, return to work or rehabilitation of disabled workforce is of immense significance as it assists employees to participate in work and earn a livelihood, contributing to the organisation's growth while also recuperating.
